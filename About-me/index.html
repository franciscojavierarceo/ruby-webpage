A Little About Me

I am a current resident of the great City of New York! I work in the field of data science; a field highly focused on empirical research. I am currently pursuing my second master's in Data Science at Columbia University. I completed my first graduate degree at Clemson University in Economics and Statistics in 2011 and my undergraduate degree in Economics at Illinois State University in 2010, where I was the President and Founder of the University's Boxing Club.

I was born of immigrant parents from Mexico. I, myself, am a natural-born citizen of the United States. I am an elementary school graduate of the Chicago Public School system and a high school graduate of Marian Catholic High School.

I am a 27 year old Chicagoan, born on the South-East side of the city. I'm a White Sox baseball fan. I love statistical theory, mathematics, and machine learning. I spend much of my time reading, writing, playing guitar, singing songs, watching the occasional movie, exercising, and spending time with my family.
